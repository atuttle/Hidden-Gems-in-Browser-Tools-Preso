Hidden Gems » Console


Pre-requisite: Everyone knows about console.log, right?


console.log(foo);

Console » Everywhere!

In any tab other than the console, hit Esc to open a mini-console. (Chrome, Safari)

Console » Labels!

console.log() takes multiple arguments. Label your debug output! (All)


console.log('foo:', foo);

//also handy!
console.info('info:', foo);
console.warn('warn:', foo);
console.error('error:', foo);
console.assert(false, 'my assertion');

Console » KB Shortcuts

Up + Down arrow keys scroll through command history

Console » Settings

Persist contents after redirect

  • Chrome: Right-click in console
  • Firefox: Button in console toolbar

Console » Dealing w/ Frames

  • Chrome: Dropdown
  • Firefox:
cd(window.frames['frameName']);

Console » Copy to Clipboard

Copy the result of a console command to the clipboard: (Chrome, FF, Safari)


copy('I\'m in ur clipboard, pastin ur text!');

Console » Math

Just type in your math problem and get the answer:


Math.sqrt(1787569);
